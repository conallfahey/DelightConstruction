About Delight

Delight Construction is an owner-operated custom home builder serving Missoula, Seely Lake, Bonner, Deer Lodge, and Western Montana. Licensed and insured, we're onsite from foundation to finish, ensuring every detail meets our exacting standards.

Our hands-on approach means transparent bids, schedule discipline, and direct accountability. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through clear communication, Montana-ready craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to quality.
